About Me

The Consultant Who's Been On the Ground — Across India & Beyond

I'm Pratik Mahadik — an ERP Functional Consultant based in Mumbai, with 4+ years of hands-on experience delivering ERP implementations that actually stick. I started my career in an automotive manufacturing plant (Mahindra CIE, Pune), operating CNC machines and understanding production workflows from the ground up.

That shop-floor foundation is what makes my consulting different. When I configure a batch manufacturing workflow or set up a QA/QC module, I know what that process looks like in reality — not just on paper. That's why my go-lives don't fail.

Today I work at the intersection of ERP implementation, AI business analysis, and workflow automation — helping manufacturing companies, pharma businesses, and real estate firms build systems that scale. I've delivered implementations on-site across West Bengal, Himachal Pradesh, Chandigarh, Maharashtra, and internationally in Nepal and Yemen — and remotely for EdTech clients across Kolkata, Delhi, Patna, Mizoram, Bengaluru, Mumbai and more.
