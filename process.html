Common Questions

FAQ

How long does a typical ERP implementation take?

It depends on the scope and number of modules. A focused single-module implementation can take 4–6 weeks. A full multi-module deployment covering purchase, inventory, production, finance and sales typically takes 8–16 weeks. I'll give you a realistic timeline after the discovery call.

Do you work on-site or remotely?

Both. I've delivered on-site implementations across Maharashtra, West Bengal, Himachal Pradesh, Chandigarh and internationally in Nepal. I've also delivered fully remote implementations including an international pharma client in Yemen. We'll decide the right approach based on your project needs.

Which ERP systems do you work with?

I work with cloud-based and EXE-based ERP systems across multiple vendors. My expertise is functional — I understand the business processes deeply and can work across different ERP platforms. I also have specific experience with Progen ERP, Zoho CRM integrations, and Azure-based systems.

What industries have you worked in?

Manufacturing, pharmaceuticals (including GMP-compliant deployments), real estate, construction and EdTech. Each industry has unique requirements — pharma needs batch tracking and QA/QC, real estate needs project-wise cost accounting, manufacturing needs production planning and BOM management.

What's your pricing structure?

Project-based pricing depending on scope, modules and duration. ERP audits start from ₹20,000. Full implementations range from ₹50,000 to ₹1,50,000 depending on complexity. Monthly retainers available from ₹30,000/month. I provide a detailed scope and quote after the free discovery call — no surprises.

Can you train our team in Marathi?

Yes — I'm a native Marathi speaker and deliver training in both English and Marathi. For teams in Maharashtra, Marathi training significantly improves adoption rates, especially for shop-floor and store teams.
